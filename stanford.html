Welcome to our Timtim ;)

Hi!

We are Timtim por Timtim. In brazilian portuguese, that's an expression used when something is explained in details. We embraced it as our name to create a collaborative system of tech classes for older adults.

We help digital immigrants use new technologies

We develop content and material to help older adults get familiar with new technologies and feel part of our communities once again

We create content and courses for people who weren't born with a smartphone or tablet in front of their eyes. Our goal is to make digital immigrants feel part of our communities by getting familiar with up and coming tech.

Our proposal is a collaborative and free system of classes for those who seek to stay up to date :)

And companies to build a more accessible digital future.

While we introduce new tools to people, our commitment is also to ensure that new products get developed so that this group of people can still use services they love.

We work alongside a initiative called Sabi, whose goal is to help companies build new digital products that empower users to master their digital gadgets.

Get in touch!

How our service works

People visit our website and watch videos or download step-by-step guides of how to use digital and physical interfaces. This is a starting point to helping their friends and relatives use digital tools.

All lessons are created by our network of collaborators and are available in a printable booklet format so that you can download them and print at home. We also test the material and the solutions created by our partners, offering usability reports to help them keep developing the best products in the market :)
